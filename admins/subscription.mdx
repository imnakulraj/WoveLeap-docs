title: Subscription & Billing
description: How to manage your WoveLeap subscription plan and billing.
---

## Viewing your plan

Go to **Settings → Subscription** to see:
- Your current plan and its features
- Number of active users, partners, and customers vs. your plan limits
- Next billing date and amount
- Payment history

## Upgrading or changing plan

1. Go to **Settings → Subscription**
2. Click **Change Plan**
3. Select the plan you want to move to
4. Confirm — upgrade takes effect immediately, downgrade takes effect at the end of your billing period

## Payments

WoveLeap uses Razorpay for secure payment processing. Supported payment methods:
- Credit / Debit card
- UPI
- Net Banking
- Wallets

A GST invoice is automatically generated for every successful payment and available for download from the Payment History section.

## Trial period

New organisations start with a free trial. During the trial you have access to all features. Your admin (WoveLeap team) may set your trial end date — you will see a countdown banner when the trial is ending.

At the end of the trial, you will need to upgrade to a paid plan to continue using the app.

## Plan limits

Plans limit the number of:
- Partners
- Customers
- Users (sales persons + admins)

If you approach a limit, you will see a warning. Upgrade your plan to increase limits.

## Cancellation

To cancel your subscription, contact WoveLeap support. Data is retained for 90 days after cancellation in case you wish to reactivate.
