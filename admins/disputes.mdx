title: Disputes
description: How to handle commission disputes raised by partners or sales persons.
---

## What is a dispute?

A dispute is raised when a partner or sales person believes a commission calculation is incorrect — wrong rate applied, incorrect deal value recorded, or a sale that should have been approved was rejected.

## Viewing disputes

Go to **Disputes** in the sidebar to see all open and resolved disputes.

Each dispute shows:
- Who raised it
- Which sale or commission it relates to
- The reason provided
- Current status (Open / Under Review / Resolved)

## Resolving a dispute

1. Open the dispute
2. Review the related sale and commission details
3. Communicate with the submitter via the dispute notes / messaging
4. Take action — either:
   - **Adjust the commission** — correct the amount and re-approve
   - **Uphold the original decision** — explain the reason in the notes
5. Mark the dispute as **Resolved**

## Preventing disputes

- Ensure commission contracts are clearly communicated to partners before they start submitting sales
- Add a rejection reason whenever rejecting a sale — vague rejections lead to more disputes
- Keep exchange rates and tax settings up to date so calculated amounts match expectations
