## Payout workflow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Commissions accumulate">
    As sales are approved, commissions are added to each partner's or sales person's pending payout balance.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review pending payouts">
    Go to **Payouts** → **Pending** to see who is owed what and for which approved sales.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Release payouts">
    Select the payouts to release and click **Release**. This marks them as paid and generates the payout record.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download transfer files">
    Download the NEFT file (India) or Wise batch CSV to process the actual bank transfers.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Releasing a payout

1. Go to **Payouts** in the sidebar
2. Switch to the **Pending** tab
3. Review each entry — you can expand to see which commissions are included
4. Select payouts to release (individual or bulk)
5. Click **Release Selected**

The payout status changes to **Released** and the partner receives an email notification.

## Downloading bank transfer files

After releasing:

**NEFT (India domestic):**
- Click **Download NEFT File** on the Payouts page
- Upload the generated file to your bank's bulk payment portal

**Wise (International):**
- Click **Download Wise CSV**
- Upload to Wise Business → Batch Payments

## GST invoices

If your org is GST-registered, a GST invoice is automatically generated for each payout. Partners can download their invoice from their Payout History. You can also access all invoices from the admin Payouts page.

## TDS deduction

If TDS applies to a partner or sales person (based on their tax settings), the deduction is calculated and shown separately. The net payout amount after TDS is what gets transferred.

At year-end, Form 16A / 26Q reports can be downloaded from **Reports → Tax Reports**.

## Payout history

The **History** tab on the Payouts page shows all past payouts with date, amount, and status. Click any entry to see the commission breakdown included in that payout.
