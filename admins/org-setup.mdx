## Accessing org setup

Go to **Settings → Organisation Setup** in the sidebar. This is where all organisation-level configuration lives.

## Company details

- **Organisation name** — displayed across the app and on PDFs
- **Address** — used on invoices and payout statements
- **Currency** — your organisation's base currency (affects all deal values and commission calculations)
- **Logo** — upload your company logo for branded PDFs and emails

## Tax & compliance (India)

If your organisation is based in India:

| Field | Description |
|---|---|
| GSTIN | Your GST registration number |
| PAN | Organisation PAN |
| GST Registration type | Regular / Composition / Unregistered |
| State | Your registered state (determines GST type: CGST+SGST vs IGST) |
| TDS settings | Whether to deduct TDS on partner / sales person payouts |

## Number series

Configure invoice and payout statement numbering formats:
- Prefix (e.g. `INV-`, `PAY-`)
- Starting number
- Financial year reset (auto-resets to 001 each April 1 for India)

Go to **Settings → Number Series** to configure.

## Email / SMTP

WoveLeap can send transactional emails (invites, password resets, payout notifications) via:
- Your own SMTP server
- SendGrid
- Amazon SES

Go to **Settings → Email Settings** and enter your SMTP credentials. Send a test email to verify.

## Payout schedule

Configure when payouts are processed: weekly, bi-weekly, or monthly. This is informational for admins — payouts are still released manually.

## Multi-currency

If your organisation works with partners in multiple countries, enable multi-currency support under **Settings → Currency**. Exchange rates are fetched automatically and can be manually overridden.
