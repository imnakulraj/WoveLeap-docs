title: Admin Overview
description: What admins can do and how to get started managing your organisation in WoveLeap.
---

As an admin, you have full control over your organisation's commission operations in WoveLeap — from onboarding your team to releasing payouts and pulling reports.

## Key responsibilities

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="User Management" icon="users">
    Invite partners, sales persons, and other admins. Set roles and SSO email addresses.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Contracts & Tiers" icon="file-contract">
    Create commission contracts with flat rates, tiered structures, or scenario-based rules.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Approvals" icon="check-circle">
    Review and approve or reject submitted sales from partners and sales persons.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payouts" icon="money-bill-wave">
    Release payouts, download bank transfer files (NEFT/Wise), and generate payout reports.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Reports" icon="chart-bar">
    Pull commission reports, ledger exports, accounting files (Tally, QuickBooks, Zoho).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Settings" icon="gear">
    Configure SMTP email, tax rules, GST/TDS, number series, and integrations.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Getting started as an admin

<Steps>
  <Step title="Complete org setup">
    On first login, you will be guided through the organisation setup wizard — company details, GST/tax info, and branding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your team">
    Go to **User Management** and invite your partners and sales persons.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up contracts">
    Go to **Contracts** and create commission contracts for your partners and sales persons.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure payouts">
    Set up payout schedules and bank details under **Settings → Payout Settings**.
  </Step>
</Steps>
