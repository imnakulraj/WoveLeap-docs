title: Payout Statements
description: How to view and download your payout statements.
---

## Viewing payout history

Go to **Payouts** in the sidebar to see all payouts — past and pending.

Each payout entry shows:
- Payout date
- Amount (in your payout currency)
- Sales included in this payout
- Status (Pending / Released / Paid)

## Downloading a statement

1. Find the payout entry you want
2. Click the **Download PDF** button on that row
3. A formatted payout statement downloads to your device

The PDF statement includes:
- Your name and organisation details
- List of approved sales covered by this payout
- Individual commission amounts and totals
- TDS / tax deductions (if applicable)
- Net payout amount

## GST Invoice

If your organisation is GST-registered and your admin has configured invoicing, a **GST Invoice PDF** is also available for each payout. Click the **Invoice** button on the payout row to download it.

## Tax deductions (TDS)

If TDS applies to your account (typically for India-based payouts), the deduction will be shown separately on your statement. Your admin can provide your TDS certificates (Form 16A) at year-end if required.

<Note>
  Statements are generated only for payouts that have been released by your admin. Pending payouts do not yet have a downloadable statement.
</Note>
