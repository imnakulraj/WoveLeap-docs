---
title: Bank Details
description: How to add or update your bank account for payouts.
---

Your admin uses the bank details you provide to process payouts. Make sure they are correct before your first payout is released.

## Adding bank details

1. Go to **Settings** (bottom of the sidebar) or your profile page
2. Find the **Bank Details** section
3. Fill in:
   - Account holder name
   - Bank name
   - Account number
   - IFSC code (India) / SWIFT / Routing number (international)
4. Click **Save**

## Updating bank details

Follow the same steps above — entering new details will replace the existing ones. Notify your admin after updating so they can verify before the next payout.

<Warning>
  If a payout is already in progress when you change your bank details, the current payout will use the old details. The new details take effect from the next payout.
</Warning>

## International payouts (Wise)

If your organisation uses Wise for international payouts, your admin may ask for your Wise account email or bank transfer details in a specific format. Your admin will guide you on what is needed.
