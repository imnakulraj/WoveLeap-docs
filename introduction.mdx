WoveLeap is a commission management platform that helps businesses track sales, calculate commissions, manage payouts, and keep everyone — partners, sales persons, and admins — on the same page.

## What you can do with WoveLeap

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Submit Sales" icon="arrow-up-right-dots">
    Partners and sales persons log deals directly in the platform. Commissions are calculated automatically.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Track Commissions" icon="chart-line">
    See a real-time breakdown of earned commissions, pending approvals, and paid amounts.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Manage Payouts" icon="money-bill-transfer">
    Admins approve and release payouts. Partners download statements as PDFs.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Stay Compliant" icon="file-invoice">
    GST invoices, TDS deductions, Form 26Q, and payout reports generated automatically.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## Who uses WoveLeap?

| Role | What they do |
|---|---|
| **Partner** | Submits sales, tracks commissions, downloads payout statements |
| **Sales Person** | Submits internal sales, views commission breakdown |
| **Admin** | Manages users, contracts, approvals, payouts, and reports |
| **Super Admin** | Platform-level management (Wove internal use only) |

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Log in">
    Go to [app.woveleap.com](https://app.woveleap.com) and sign in with your username and password — or use Google / Microsoft SSO if your admin has enabled it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete org setup">
    First-time admins will be guided through a one-time organisation setup before reaching the dashboard.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Invite your team">
    Admins can invite partners and sales persons from the User Management page.
  </Step>
</Steps>
