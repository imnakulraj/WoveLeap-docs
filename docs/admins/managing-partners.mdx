## Partners page

Go to **Partners** in the sidebar to see all partners in your organisation.

Each partner entry shows:
- Name and contact details
- Assigned commission contract
- Total sales submitted and approved
- Total commissions earned
- Payout currency

## Editing a partner

1. Click on a partner's name to open their profile
2. Update any fields — name, email, payout currency, bank details, SSO email, PAN/GST (for India)
3. Click **Save**

## Assigning a contract

Each partner must have a commission contract assigned before their commissions can be calculated.

1. Open the partner's profile
2. In the **Contract** field, select the relevant contract from the dropdown
3. Save

If no suitable contract exists yet, create one first under **Contracts**.

## Partner bank details

Bank details for a partner can be entered by:
- The partner themselves (via Settings in their account)
- The admin (via the partner's profile in User Management / Partners)

Bank details are used when generating NEFT or Wise payout files.

## Deactivating a partner

If a partner is no longer active:
1. Open their profile
2. Click **Deactivate**

Deactivated partners cannot log in but their historical data is preserved.
