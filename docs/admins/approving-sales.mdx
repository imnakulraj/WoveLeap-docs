## Where to review submissions

Go to **Sales Workflow** (or **Commissions → Pending Approvals**) in the sidebar. All submitted sales waiting for review are listed here.

## Approving a sale

1. Click on a pending submission to open the detail view
2. Review the customer details, deal value, product, and any attached documents
3. Click **Approve**
4. The commission is calculated automatically and the partner / sales person is notified

## Rejecting a sale

1. Open the submission
2. Click **Reject**
3. Enter a rejection reason — this is shown to the submitter so they understand what needs correction
4. Click **Confirm Reject**

The submitter can then correct and resubmit if appropriate.

## Bulk approval

If you have many pending submissions:
1. Use the checkboxes to select multiple rows
2. Click **Bulk Approve** at the top of the list

:::warning
Use bulk approval only when you have already reviewed the submissions — it approves all selected at once without an individual confirmation step.
:::

## Sales from partners vs. internal sales persons

Both appear in the same approval queue. You can filter by **Submitted by** to separate partner submissions from internal sales person submissions.

## Editing before approval

If the deal value or details need a minor correction:
1. Open the submission
2. Click **Edit**
3. Make changes and save
4. Then approve

Notify the submitter of any changes you made.
