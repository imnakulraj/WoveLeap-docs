As an admin, you have full control over your organisation's commission operations in WoveLeap — from onboarding your team to releasing payouts and pulling reports.

## Key responsibilities

| Area | What you do |
|---|---|
| **User Management** | Invite partners, sales persons, and other admins. Set roles and SSO email addresses. |
| **Contracts & Tiers** | Create commission contracts with flat rates, tiered structures, or scenario-based rules. |
| **Approvals** | Review and approve or reject submitted sales from partners and sales persons. |
| **Payouts** | Release payouts, download bank transfer files (NEFT/Wise), and generate payout reports. |
| **Reports** | Pull commission reports, ledger exports, accounting files (Tally, QuickBooks, Zoho). |
| **Settings** | Configure SMTP email, tax rules, GST/TDS, number series, and integrations. |

## Getting started as an admin

**Step 1 — Complete org setup**

On first login, you will be guided through the organisation setup wizard — company details, GST/tax info, and branding.

**Step 2 — Invite your team**

Go to **User Management** and invite your partners and sales persons.

**Step 3 — Set up contracts**

Go to **Contracts** and create commission contracts for your partners and sales persons.

**Step 4 — Configure payouts**

Set up payout schedules and bank details under **Settings → Payout Settings**.
