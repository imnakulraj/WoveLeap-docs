WoveLeap is a commission management platform that helps businesses track sales, calculate commissions, manage payouts, and keep everyone — partners, sales persons, and admins — on the same page.

## What you can do with WoveLeap

| Feature | Description |
|---|---|
| **Submit Sales** | Partners and sales persons log deals directly in the platform. Commissions are calculated automatically. |
| **Track Commissions** | See a real-time breakdown of earned commissions, pending approvals, and paid amounts. |
| **Manage Payouts** | Admins approve and release payouts. Partners download statements as PDFs. |
| **Stay Compliant** | GST invoices, TDS deductions, Form 26Q, and payout reports generated automatically. |

## Who uses WoveLeap?

| Role | What they do |
|---|---|
| **Partner** | Submits sales, tracks commissions, downloads payout statements |
| **Sales Person** | Submits internal sales, views commission breakdown |
| **Admin** | Manages users, contracts, approvals, payouts, and reports |

## Getting started

**Step 1 — Log in**

Go to [app.woveleap.com](https://app.woveleap.com) and sign in with your username and password — or use Google / Microsoft SSO if your admin has enabled it.

**Step 2 — Complete org setup**

First-time admins will be guided through a one-time organisation setup before reaching the dashboard.

**Step 3 — Invite your team**

Admins can invite partners and sales persons from the User Management page.
