WoveLeap supports Single Sign-On (SSO) with **Google** and **Microsoft** so you can log in without remembering a separate password.

## Before you start

Your admin must register your Google or Microsoft email address against your WoveLeap username. If the SSO buttons are greyed out or you see an error, contact your admin.

## How to sign in with SSO

1. Go to [app.woveleap.com](https://app.woveleap.com)
2. Enter your **WoveLeap username** in the username field (required before SSO buttons become active)
3. Click **Google** or **Microsoft** — a sign-in popup will open
4. Complete sign-in in the popup using the account your admin registered
5. The popup closes and you are logged in automatically

:::info
You must sign in with the **exact** Google or Microsoft account your admin has registered for you. If you have multiple accounts in your browser, use the correct one matching your registered email.
:::

## Common errors

**'No Google/Microsoft email registered for this username'**

Your admin has not yet added an SSO email to your account. Ask them to go to **User Management**, find your account, and add your Google or Microsoft email address.

---

**'Wrong account signed in'**

You completed the SSO popup using a different account than the one registered. Sign out of Google or Microsoft in your browser, then try again with the correct account.

---

**Sign-in popup was blocked**

Allow popups for `app.woveleap.com` in your browser settings and try again.

---

**'Sign-in was cancelled'**

The popup was closed before completing. Click the Google or Microsoft button again to retry.
