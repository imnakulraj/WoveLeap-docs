## Sidebar

The sidebar on the left is your main navigation. What you see depends on your role.

| Section | Available to |
|---|---|
| Dashboard | All roles |
| Sales & Commissions | Admin, Sales Person |
| Partner Portal | Partners |
| Commissions | Admin |
| Payouts | Admin, Partner, Sales Person |
| Reports & Analytics | Admin |
| User Management | Admin |
| Settings | Admin |

On mobile, tap the **hamburger menu** (≡) at the top left to open the sidebar.

## Dashboard

The Dashboard gives you a snapshot of activity:
- **Admins** see total commissions, pending approvals, payout summary, and recent sales
- **Partners / Sales Persons** see their own earnings, submitted sales, and pending commissions

## Top bar

The top bar shows:
- Your organisation name
- Notification bell (for approvals and alerts)
- Your profile / logout button

## Settings

Accessible from the bottom of the sidebar. Admins can configure organisation details, SMTP email, tax settings, number series, and integrations here.
