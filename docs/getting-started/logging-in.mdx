## Standard login

1. Go to [app.woveleap.com](https://app.woveleap.com)
2. Enter your **Username** and **Password**
3. Click **Sign In**

Your username is set by your admin when your account is created. If you have forgotten your password, use the **Forgot Password** link on the login page — a reset link will be sent to your registered email.

:::info
Your username is **not** your email address. It is a short identifier (e.g. `john.smith`) assigned by your organisation's admin.
:::

## Two-factor authentication (2FA)

If your organisation has enabled 2FA:

1. Enter your username and password as normal
2. Open your authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, etc.)
3. Enter the 6-digit code shown for WoveLeap
4. Click **Verify**

Codes expire every 30 seconds — if entry fails, wait for the next code and try again.

## Forgot your password?

1. Click **Forgot password?** on the login page
2. Enter the email address registered to your account
3. Check your inbox for a reset link (check spam if it doesn't arrive within a few minutes)
4. Click the link, set a new password, and log in

:::warning
Password reset links expire after 1 hour. Request a new one if yours has expired.
:::

## First-time login

When your admin invites you, you will receive an email with a temporary password or a direct login link. Use it to log in, then change your password from **Settings → Security**.
