### I forgot my username. What do I do?

Contact your organisation's admin — they can look up your username in User Management and share it with you.

---

### I didn't receive my password reset email.

Check your spam / junk folder. If it's not there, your admin can manually reset your password from User Management. Also confirm the email address you used matches what was registered for your account.

---

### Why is the Google / Microsoft button greyed out?

You need to enter your username in the username field first. The SSO buttons only activate once a username is provided. If they remain inactive after entering your username, your admin may not have registered an SSO email for your account yet.

---

### My commission amount looks wrong. What should I do?

First, check the commission breakdown on the Commissions page to see which rate was applied. If you believe the rate or deal value is incorrect, raise a dispute from the relevant commission entry or contact your admin directly.

---

### How long does approval take?

Approval timelines depend on your organisation's process. Contact your admin if a submission has been pending for longer than expected.

---

### Can I change my payout currency?

You can update your payout currency in Settings. However, payouts that have already been released will not be affected — only future payouts will use the new currency. Inform your admin when changing.

---

### I submitted a sale with the wrong deal value. Can I edit it?

If the sale is still **Pending**, you can edit it from the Partner Portal or Sales Workflow. If it has already been approved, contact your admin — they can adjust the value and re-calculate the commission.

---

### Why does my commission show 'Pending' even after my sale was approved?

Commission status moves to **Paid** only after your admin releases the payout. Approved commissions sit in **Pending** until the next payout run. Check your admin's payout schedule for when to expect it.

---

### How do I enable 2FA on my account?

Go to **Settings → Security** and follow the instructions to link an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, or similar).

---

### I'm an admin — can I see a partner's full payout breakdown?

Yes. Go to **Payouts**, find the partner's payout entry, and expand it to see all commissions included. You can also download their payout statement PDF from the same row.
