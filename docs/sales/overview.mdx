As a sales person, WoveLeap tracks your deals, calculates your commissions automatically, and shows your payout history — no spreadsheets needed.

## Your workflow

**1. Submit a sale**

Log a completed deal from the Sales Workflow page. Enter the customer, deal value, and product.

**2. Manager reviews**

Your admin or manager reviews the submission and approves or rejects it.

**3. Commission calculated**

On approval, your commission is automatically calculated based on your assigned contract.

**4. Payout released**

Your admin releases your payout on a scheduled basis (weekly, monthly, etc.). You can track it in Payout History.

## Key pages

| Page | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Sales Workflow | Submit and track your sales submissions |
| Commissions | Your earned commissions per sale |
| Payout History | Past and upcoming payouts |
