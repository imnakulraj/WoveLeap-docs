## Commissions page

Go to **Commissions** in the sidebar to see your full commission history.

Each entry shows:
- The sale it relates to
- Deal value
- Your commission amount
- Status (Pending / Approved / Paid)

## How commissions are calculated

Commissions are calculated automatically when a sale is approved, based on the contract your admin has assigned to you. This may be a flat percentage, a tiered rate, or a target-based structure. Your admin can explain the specifics of your contract.

## Monthly performance

Go to **Monthly Performance** to see a summary of your commissions grouped by month — useful for tracking progress towards targets or reviewing past earnings.
