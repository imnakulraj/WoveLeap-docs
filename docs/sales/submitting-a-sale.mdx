## Steps

1. Go to **Sales Workflow** in the sidebar
2. Click **New Sale** or **Submit Sale**
3. Fill in the required fields:
   - **Customer** — select an existing customer or enter a new one
   - **Product / Service** — what was sold
   - **Deal value** — total deal amount
   - **Close date** — when the deal was signed
   - **Notes** (optional)
4. Click **Submit for Approval**

The sale appears in your submissions list with status **Pending**.

## Submission statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Pending | Waiting for manager review |
| Approved | Commission will be calculated |
| Rejected | See rejection note — resubmit if corrected |
| Paid | Commission has been paid |

## Editing a submission

You can edit a **Pending** submission before it is reviewed. Once it is approved or rejected, contact your admin for corrections.

:::tip
If your deal involves a partner, your admin may link the sale to a partner record — you don't need to do this yourself.
:::
