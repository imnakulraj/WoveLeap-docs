As a partner, WoveLeap is your portal for submitting sales, tracking commissions, and downloading payout statements — all in one place.

## Your workflow

**1. Submit a sale**

Log a new deal through the Partner Portal. Provide customer details, deal value, and any required documents.

**2. Admin reviews**

Your admin reviews the submitted sale. You will be notified when it is approved or if changes are needed.

**3. Commission calculated**

Once approved, your commission is calculated based on the contract your admin has set up for you.

**4. Payout released**

When your admin releases the payout, you receive a notification. You can download your payout statement as a PDF.

## Key pages

| Page | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Partner Portal | Submit and track your sales |
| Commissions | Your earned commissions broken down by sale |
| Payout History | All past and pending payouts |
| Settings | Your bank details and profile |
