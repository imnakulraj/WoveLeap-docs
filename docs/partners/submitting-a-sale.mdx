## Steps

1. Log in and go to **Partner Portal** in the sidebar
2. Click **Submit New Sale**
3. Fill in the sale details:
   - **Customer name** — the company or individual you are bringing in
   - **Deal value** — the total deal amount in the applicable currency
   - **Product / Service** — select from the list your admin has configured
   - **Notes** (optional) — any additional context for the admin
4. Upload any required documents (contract, PO, email confirmation) if prompted
5. Click **Submit**

Your sale will appear in the **My Submissions** list with status **Pending**.

## Submission statuses

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Pending | Submitted, awaiting admin review |
| Under Review | Admin is actively reviewing |
| Approved | Approved — commission calculated |
| Rejected | Not approved — see the rejection note for reason |
| Paid | Commission has been paid out |

## After submitting

You will receive an email notification when your sale is approved or rejected. If rejected, the admin's note explains why — you can resubmit with corrections if needed.

:::tip
Double-check the deal value and currency before submitting. Once approved, corrections require admin intervention.
:::
