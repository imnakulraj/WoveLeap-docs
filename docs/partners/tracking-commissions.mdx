## Commissions page

Go to **Commissions** in the sidebar to see all commissions associated with your account.

Each row shows:
- Sale date and customer name
- Deal value
- Commission rate applied
- Commission amount earned
- Current status (Pending / Approved / Paid)
- Payout currency

## Commission calculation

Your commission is calculated based on the **contract** your admin has set up for you. Contracts can include:

- **Flat rate** — a fixed percentage of the deal value
- **Tiered rate** — higher percentage as your total sales volume increases
- **Capped commission** — a maximum earning per deal or per period

You can see which contract applies to you by asking your admin. The calculated amount is always shown once a sale is approved.

## Currency

If your payout currency differs from your organisation's base currency, amounts are shown converted at the exchange rate applicable at the time of approval. Both the original and converted amounts are visible.

## Filtering

Use the **date range** and **status** filters at the top of the Commissions page to narrow down the view.
