title: Payout History
description: How to view your payout history as a sales person.
---

## Viewing payouts

Go to **Payouts** in the sidebar to see all payouts associated with your account.

Each entry shows:
- Payout date
- Total amount paid
- Currency
- Status (Pending / Released / Paid)

## Payout frequency

Your admin sets the payout schedule (weekly, bi-weekly, monthly, etc.). Check with your admin if you are unsure when to expect your next payout.

## Downloading a statement

Click **Download PDF** on any released payout to get a formatted statement showing the breakdown of commissions included in that payout.

<Note>
  If your commission is pending for longer than expected, check that the related sale has been approved. Unapproved sales are not included in payouts.
</Note>
