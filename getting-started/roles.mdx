---
title: User Roles
description: Understanding the different roles in WoveLeap and what each can do.
---

WoveLeap has four roles. Each role sees a different view of the app with access only to what is relevant to them.

## Partner

An external partner (reseller, referral agent, distributor) who brings in business.

**Can:**
- Submit sales through the Partner Portal
- View their own commission breakdown
- Track approval status of submitted sales
- Download payout statements as PDFs
- Update their bank details for payouts

**Cannot:**
- See other partners' data
- Approve or reject sales
- Access admin settings

## Sales Person

An internal sales team member.

**Can:**
- Submit sales assigned to their account
- View their own commission calculations
- See payout history

**Cannot:**
- Access partner data
- Approve sales or release payouts

## Admin

The organisation administrator — typically a finance or operations manager.

**Can:**
- Invite and manage all users (partners, sales persons, other admins)
- Create and manage commission contracts and tier structures
- Approve or reject submitted sales
- Release payouts and download payout reports
- Configure organisation settings, tax rules, and integrations
- View all commissions, ledger entries, and analytics

## Super Admin

WoveLeap platform staff only. Manages organisations, subscription plans, and platform-level settings. Not relevant to end users.

---

<Note>
  Role permissions can be customised by your admin under **Settings → Role Permissions**.
</Note>
