---
title: Frequently Asked Questions
description: Answers to the most common questions about WoveLeap.
---

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="I forgot my username. What do I do?">
    Contact your organisation's admin — they can look up your username in User Management and share it with you.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I didn't receive my password reset email.">
    Check your spam / junk folder. If it's not there, your admin can manually reset your password from User Management. Also confirm the email address you used matches what was registered for your account.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why is the Google / Microsoft button greyed out?">
    You need to enter your username in the username field first. The SSO buttons only activate once a username is provided. If they remain inactive after entering your username, your admin may not have registered an SSO email for your account yet.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="My commission amount looks wrong. What should I do?">
    First, check the commission breakdown on the Commissions page to see which rate was applied. If you believe the rate or deal value is incorrect, raise a dispute from the relevant commission entry or contact your admin directly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does approval take?">
    Approval timelines depend on your organisation's process — WoveLeap does not set a deadline. Contact your admin if a submission has been pending for longer than expected.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I change my payout currency?">
    You can update your payout currency in Settings. However, payouts that have already been released will not be affected — only future payouts will use the new currency. Inform your admin when changing, as it affects the bank transfer files they generate.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I submitted a sale with the wrong deal value. Can I edit it?">
    If the sale is still **Pending**, you can edit it from the Partner Portal or Sales Workflow. If it has already been approved, contact your admin — they can adjust the value and re-calculate the commission.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Why does my commission show 'Pending' even after my sale was approved?">
    Commission status moves to **Paid** only after your admin releases the payout. Approved commissions sit in **Pending** until the next payout run. Check your admin's payout schedule for when to expect it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I enable 2FA on my account?">
    Go to **Settings → Security** and follow the instructions to link an authenticator app (Google Authenticator, Authy, or similar). Your admin can also enforce 2FA for all users in the organisation.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="I'm an admin — can I see a partner's full payout breakdown?">
    Yes. Go to **Payouts**, find the partner's payout entry, and expand it to see all commissions included. You can also download their payout statement PDF from the same row.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>
