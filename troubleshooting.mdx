## Login issues

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="'Invalid username or password'">
    - Check that Caps Lock is off
    - Remember your username is not your email (it's a short ID like `john.smith`)
    - Use **Forgot Password** to reset if needed
    - Contact your admin if you are locked out
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="'Your account has been deactivated'">
    Your admin has deactivated your account. Contact them to reactivate it.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="2FA code not working">
    - Ensure your device's clock is set to automatic / network time — authenticator apps rely on exact time sync
    - Wait for the next 30-second cycle and try the new code
    - If the problem persists, your admin can disable 2FA on your account so you can log in and re-enrol
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="SSO popup blocked">
    Allow popups for `app.woveleap.com` in your browser:
    - **Chrome:** click the popup-blocked icon in the address bar → Allow
    - **Safari:** Preferences → Websites → Pop-up Windows → Allow for this site
    - **Firefox:** click Options in the blocked popup bar → Allow popups
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Commission issues

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Commission not calculated after approval">
    The partner or sales person may not have a contract assigned. Go to their profile in User Management and assign the relevant contract, then re-approve the sale.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Wrong currency shown">
    Check the user's **Payout Currency** setting in their profile. If it's incorrect, update it — subsequent commissions will use the correct currency. Past entries may need manual correction by your admin.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Email issues

<AccordionGroup>

  <Accordion title="Users not receiving invite or notification emails">
    Go to **Settings → Email Settings** and verify your SMTP configuration. Click **Send Test Email** — if it fails, check your SMTP credentials, host, and port. Ensure port 587 (or 465 for SSL) is not blocked by your server firewall.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Password reset link not working">
    Reset links expire after 1 hour. Ask the user to request a new reset link. If emails are not arriving at all, see the SMTP troubleshooting step above.
  </Accordion>

</AccordionGroup>

## Still need help?

Contact WoveLeap support at **support@woveleap.com** with a description of the issue, your organisation name, and any error messages you see.
